My Family

Our family is a small and happy family. I have two brothers (Reuben and Diego Jr.) and four sisters(Agapita, Daisy, Blessie and Ligalia). I have one nephew and one niece. Three of my sisters has their own family now.

I have so many cousins and keens on my parents' both sides. The "Bejoc" clan alone has populated one barangay of keens in Cebu, and many other have scattered in different places in Visayas and Mindanao. My father's side is a native of Mindanao, tracing back to my grandparents; they were both a mixed blood of Muslim and Erumanen ne Menuvu (one of the Indigenous Peoples' group in Mindanao).
